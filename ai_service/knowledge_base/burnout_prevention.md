# Burnout Prevention and Cognitive Rest Heuristics

## Identifying Cognitive Fatigue
Cognitive fatigue accumulates during high focus cycles. Warning signs:
- Decaying memory retrieval during spaced reviews.
- High error rates on medium-difficulty LeetCode problems.
- Loss of daily habit consistency despite freeze availability.

## Stress Buffer Protocols
- **Mandatory Screen-Free Walk**: Take a 15-minute walk outside. Do not look at your phone. This triggers optic flow, which lowers activity in the amygdala and reduces stress.
- **Micro-Breaks**: Stand up, stretch, and hydrate for 3-5 minutes every 30 minutes of deep focus.
- **Burnout Gauge**: GrowthOS calculates burnout risk based on your average study minutes. Keep daily focus hours under 2.5 hours unless preparing for near deadlines.

## Sleep Hygiene for Learning
Sleep is when memory consolidation occurs! If you study intensely but sleep poorly, your neural connections for that day's material will not solidify. 
- Avoid coding or debugging past 10:00 PM.
- Expose eyes to bright natural light within 30 minutes of waking to set your circadian cortisol rhythm.
